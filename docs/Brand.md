# Brand Guidelines

## Brand Name

Your Page 有頁網頁設計顧問


## Brand Positioning

Your Page is a strategic website design consultant.

We help businesses transform websites from simple online presence into business growth tools.


---

# Brand Personality

The brand should feel:

- Professional
- Strategic
- Modern
- Reliable
- Minimal
- Premium


Avoid:

- Cheap
- Over-promotional
- Trend chasing
- Excessive decoration


---

# Visual Direction

Style:

Urban Minimalism

Design principles:

- Clean layouts
- Strong hierarchy
- Professional typography
- Purposeful whitespace
- Premium feeling


---

# Color Direction

Primary feeling:

Deep blue-grey

Represents:

- Trust
- Technology
- Professionalism


Supporting feeling:

Warm cream

Represents:

- Human touch
- Premium experience


Accent:

Orange / Coral

Represents:

- Energy
- Action
- Conversion


---

# Communication Style

Tone:

- Professional but approachable
- Clear and educational
- Strategic rather than sales-focused


Avoid:

- Aggressive sales language
- Over-promising
- Fake urgency


---

# Content Philosophy

Content should:

- Educate customers
- Build trust
- Demonstrate expertise
- Encourage business improvement


The goal is not only attention.

The goal is qualified leads.


---

# Design Principle

Every design decision must answer:

"Does this help the customer's business goal?"

Beauty without purpose is not enough.
