# Decision 001

## Date

2026-07-23

## Topic

Company Positioning


## Decision

Your Page will position itself as a strategic website design consultant instead of a low-cost website production service.


## Reason

The low-price website market has high competition and limited differentiation.

A consulting-based approach allows higher value creation through:

- Strategy
- User experience
- Branding
- Conversion optimization


## Impact

Affects:

- Pricing
- Marketing content
- Sales strategy
- Website design direction


## Future Review

Review when:

- Customer demand changes
- New service models appear
- Market positioning requires adjustment

# Decision 002

## Decision

- Roles
- Workflows
- SOPs

## Impact
