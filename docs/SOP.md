# Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)


## Purpose

Define repeatable processes for Your Page operations.


---

# SOP Design Principles


Every SOP should include:

- Trigger
- Input
- Process
- Output
- Owner
- Improvement Opportunity


---

# Client Acquisition SOP


## Trigger

New potential client identified.


## Input

- Company information
- Industry
- Website status
- Contact information


## Process


Step 1:
Add lead into CRM.


Step 2:
Research client background.


Step 3:
Analyze website opportunity.


Step 4:
Create personalized outreach.


Step 5:
Track response.
